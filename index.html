Men on Mission

Men on Mission is an interdenominational Christian platform instituted to serve the body of Christ by addressing fatherhood and by extension the family. Man is the foundation of God creation. No father no nation, the quality of fathers determine the quality of a nation. This is the burden of Men on Mission. It was commissioned in 2016 and since increase in impact. We run seminars, training for fathers and couples, we counsels and mentor the future fathers.

Board Of Trustee

ENGR. ISAAC OMOSULE NENUWA

Is the chairman board of trustee of Men on Mission. He is raised from the Redeemed Christian Church of God. He is a pastor, father educator, speaker and a writer. He speaks to youths, Fathers and families. He had served in the leadership capacity of various ministries across the Nation. He is the National Administrative secretary for Fellowship of Christian Traditional Rulers of Nigeria (FECTRON) He is a licence preacher and widely travels. He is passionate to see the emergence of Godly families across the nation. He holds a master degree in Mechanical Engineering, a PGD in theology and Alumina of Institute of National Transformation .He is a lecturer at Rufus Giwa Polytechnics, Owo, Ondo state Nigeria. He is married and blessed with children.

MATTHEW OLAYINKA OLADIPO (an entrepreneur by profession)

Is the secretary of Men on Mission Board of Trustee. He was the pioneer President/Coordinator of Redeemed Christian Corpers Fellowship (RCCF) at Ondo West Local Government of Ondo State between 2012-2013.He is the current Youth President of RCCG Mercy Seat Zone at Ondo Province Four (4). He is also the Managing Director of Matmos International Limited. He is a motivational speaker. He holds M.sc in Mass Communication, PGD in Journalism, PGD in Customer Relationship Management, and Higher Diploma certificate in Mass Communication. He is also an associate member of Nigerian Institute of Public Relations (ANIPR). He is happily married and blessed with children.

OTUNLA SAMUEL OLU

A professional Accountant with the experience spans over 15 years is the Vice chairman to the Men on Mission Board of Trustee. He is a pastor with the Redeemed Christian Church of with over 19 years experience as a Minister. He had served at various Parishes as a Minister and currently Parish Pastor at City of Peace Parish in kajola, Akure Ondo Province 11. He is a God fearing, devoted, easy going and kind-hearted person. Most time he is operating behind the scene in the Church. He specialized on Auditing, a public sector and financial management accounting expert.He holds a B.Sc in Accounting, MBA and a certified National Accountant (ANA) He is happily married and blessed with wonderful Children

OJO OLUMUYIWA CURTIS

Is a member of Men on Mission board of trustee as the publicity officer. He is a professional and a gifted teacher. He was ordained as an Evangelist in Cherubim and Seraphim 1992 rose to the rank of Senior Superintendent Apostle in 2020. He teaches and minister across various Christian denomination He holds a PGDE.HND. He is happily married and blessed with children.
